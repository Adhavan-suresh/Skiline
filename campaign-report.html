Key Insights

Housewives is the star performer

₹13.9 CPL with 4.76% CTR — best metrics across all adsets. Audience resonates strongly with the creative. Scale-up candidate if budget increases.

Efficient overall CPL at ₹18.2

Industry benchmark for employment lead gen in India is ₹50–150. Skiline is running at 3–8x more efficiently. Volume is the main lever to pull.

₹1,061 wasted on 2M campaign

Misconfigured campaign with CPM of ₹8,623 generated 0 leads. Campaign is still active with no adsets — should be deleted to avoid future accidental spend.

Ashok Nagar geo test underperforming

Both AN adsets showing 31–152% higher CPL vs broad. Narrow geo increases CPM without proportional lead quality gain. Full verdict after 2 more weeks of data.

Lead quality gap is the real problem

606 leads collected, ~1 confirmed conversion. Conversion rate <0.2%. Root causes: "More Volume" form type (no friction), delayed follow-up, broad audience. Not an ads problem — a post-lead process problem.

Action Items

1

Delete the 2M campaign

Still active, no adsets, zero leads, ₹1,061 wasted. Risk of accidental future spend. Low effort, no downside.

2

Switch to Higher Intent lead forms

Current "More Volume" forms have zero friction — pre-filled fields = accidental submissions. Higher Intent adds a review step, cuts volume ~30% but removes junk leads. Improves downstream conversion rate significantly.

3

Build lookalike audience from client converter list

Client has 200+ past converters from newspaper/events/direct outreach. Upload as Custom Audience → create Special Ad Audience. Target people who behave like actual converters, not just form fillers.

4

AB test verdict — 2 weeks

Ashok Nagar adsets currently 31–152% more expensive than broad. If CPL stays 2x+ higher after full data, shut down AN test and redirect ₹200/day entirely to broad adsets.

5

Fix lead follow-up speed (off-platform)

Leads contacted within 5 minutes convert 9x better than those contacted after 1 hour. If calls are happening hours/days after submission, this is likely the #1 reason for <0.2% conversion rate.
